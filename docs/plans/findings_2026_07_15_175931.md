## Design decisions

- Multi-pool is a manual-booking capability only; scan and exact booking remain single-account.
- The Web API adds `account_mode=multi_pool` and exactly two `user_keys`, while retaining legacy `user_key`.
- Secrets are passed once through child-process stdin, not argv or account-identifying logs.
- The coordinator combines per-account pacing with a global adjacent-hour lease ledger.
- A timeout permanently tombstones that logical hour for the run, including after stable absence.
- The second account starts 0.35 seconds after the first account's adjacent-hour assignment; later writes obey both account and global backoff.
